Our story

A retreat rooted in nature & warmth

Who we are

More than a venue.
A feeling.

Ndeiya House is a holiday home with a characteristic touch of simplicity. Set on 5 acres just outside Nairobi, our property is home to a main house, private cottages, lush gardens, a swimming pool, a steam room, and man-made waterfalls.

All rooms are individually decorated with their own unique charm, furnished to excellent standards. The backyard has a swimming pool, a serene seating area, and a one-of-a-kind outdoor dining experience.

We are committed to eco-friendly practices — from our working farm to our dedication to preserving the natural landscape that makes Ndeiya House so special.
