Accommodation

We have six en-suite bedrooms that can accommodate a maximum of 14 guests. Three bedrooms have double beds, two have twin single beds, and one has a double bed plus two single beds. We do not allow extra guests beyond 14, as we do not have additional mattresses or beds.

Check-in is from 12:00 PM and check-out is by 10:00 AM. If you need a late check-out, please let us know in advance and we will do our best to accommodate you.

The rate (Ksh 47,000/night standard; Ksh 57,000/night in December) includes exclusive use of all 6 bedrooms, access to the swimming pool and steam room, the lush gardens and man-made waterfalls, WiFi, a private chef (you provide the ingredients), and backup generator. Grill services are available on request.

Catering & Food

The team building day package (Ksh 3,500 per person) includes 10am tea and snacks, lunch with a soft drink or water, and 4pm tea and snacks.

Yes — Ndeiya Farm is our on-site working farm. We grow a range of fresh produce including lettuce, broccoli, cauliflower, celery, kale, spinach, and leeks, as well as dairy and eggs. Where possible, our in-house chef uses fresh farm produce in meals. The farm is also available for educational tours.

We don't offer in-house event coordination, but we can connect you with experienced event planners we work with regularly.

Clients need to bring portable washrooms and a washroom minder.
